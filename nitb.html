CHAPTER - NIT BHOPAL

WORKS


The primary objective of this organization is to provide education to the underprivileged, illiterate children. For this purpose, we hold classes regularly for our children at a center in Anna Nagar.

For the smooth functioning and for the effective implementation of the policies, the entire workforce has been divided into a management team and a team of volunteers. Separate verticals have been designed in order to efficiently meet all the requirements of the organization.

All these departments have individual heads, who are free to take decisions regarding their vertical, and a group of volunteers assist these heads, this promotes a friendly and healthy work atmosphere.
